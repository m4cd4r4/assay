## 1. Purpose

The parties wish to exchange Confidential Information for the purpose of evaluating and performing a COBOL documentation generation engagement ("Purpose").

## 2. Definition of Confidential Information

"Confidential Information" means all information disclosed by one party to the other, whether orally, in writing, or electronically, including but not limited to:

- Source code (COBOL programs, copybooks, JCL, scripts)
- Technical documentation, system architecture, and data models
- Business logic, processing rules, and operational procedures
- Customer lists, financial data, and strategic plans
- Pricing, proposals, and engagement terms
- Any information marked or identified as confidential

Confidential Information does not include information that:
- Is or becomes publicly available through no fault of the Recipient
- Was already known to the Recipient prior to disclosure
- Is independently developed by the Recipient without reference to the Confidential Information
- Is required to be disclosed by law or regulatory authority (with prompt notice to the Discloser)

## 3. Obligations of the Recipient

The Recipient agrees to:

a) Use Confidential Information solely for the Purpose
b) Not disclose Confidential Information to any third party without prior written consent
c) Limit access to Confidential Information to personnel who need to know for the Purpose
d) Protect Confidential Information with at least the same degree of care used for its own confidential information, and no less than reasonable care
e) Not copy or reproduce Confidential Information except as necessary for the Purpose
f) Not reverse-engineer any business logic or processes from the Confidential Information

## 4. Source Code Handling (Specific to Assay Engagements)

Solaisoft Pty Ltd, as Recipient of source code, additionally agrees to:

a) Process source code exclusively via the Anthropic API under commercial terms (inputs not used for model training)
b) Transmit source code only over TLS 1.3 encrypted connections
c) Not store source code on any persistent storage beyond what is necessary for processing
d) Delete all copies of source code within **30 calendar days** of delivering the completed documentation
e) Provide written confirmation of deletion upon request
f) Limit source code access to the Director of Solaisoft Pty Ltd only

## 5. Intellectual Property

Nothing in this Agreement transfers ownership of any intellectual property. The Discloser retains all rights in its Confidential Information.

## 6. Term and Survival

This Agreement is effective from the Effective Date and continues for **2 years** from the date of last disclosure, unless terminated earlier by mutual written consent.

Confidentiality obligations survive for **3 years** after the termination or expiry of this Agreement.

## 7. Return and Destruction

Upon written request or expiry of this Agreement, the Recipient shall:

a) Return or destroy all Confidential Information in its possession
b) Provide written certification of destruction within 14 days
c) Delete all electronic copies from all systems, backups, and archives

## 8. Remedies

The parties acknowledge that breach of this Agreement may cause irreparable harm for which monetary damages may be insufficient. The injured party is entitled to seek injunctive relief in addition to any other remedies available at law.

## 9. General

a) **Governing Law:** This Agreement is governed by the laws of Western Australia, Australia.
b) **Jurisdiction:** The parties submit to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of Western Australia.
c) **Entire Agreement:** This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement regarding confidentiality and supersedes all prior agreements on the subject.
d) **Amendment:** This Agreement may only be amended in writing signed by both parties.
e) **Assignment:** Neither party may assign this Agreement without the other's written consent.
